Rectangular time

Work in 4D
Rectangular space is a portal to other worlds,
not a replacement of our own and our surroundings.

There is no binary of physical/digital,
they are happening simultaneously.

How can you make the most of both?
Get comfortable at your desk, AND on your desktop.
Rearrange the digital furniture to suit your needs.

Create moments of contrast by moving between
group/independent, online/offline, small group/larger group.
Digital movement
